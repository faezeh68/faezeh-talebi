About Me

I am a physician with about six years of experience. Most of my work experience has been as a family physician, which introduced me to the importance of data in medicine and healthcare. I have always been curious about data and was excited to use it to develop treatment plans for my patients and manage the healthcare team. This curiosity made me interested in the application of data in medicine and healthcare and led me to increase my knowledge in the field of data analysis. The more I learned, the more interested I became. Finally, I decided to start my new career as a data analyst. My passion for health data analysis is because, with the advancement of technology, expansion of electronic health records, health gadgets such as wearable devices, and health apps on smartphones, significant data in the field of health is produced daily. The value of this data is determined when it can be used to help improve the health status and quality of life, reduce the risk of treatment procedures and medication complications, and reduce the treatment cost. This is a particularly good way to help people. So here I am! I am ready to start my adventure as a Junior Data Analyst and am always ready to dive deeper into the world of data, learn more and gain experience. If you are visiting my website, I think I would be an excellent choice as a member of your team. We can work together and make the world a better place to live.
